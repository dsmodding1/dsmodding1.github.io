// LEGAL

Takedown Requests

DSModding respects intellectual property and creator rights. If you believe content shared in our community infringes on your rights, please contact us and we will review your request promptly.

CONTACT

Send takedown emails to:

seno@dsmodding.com

What to include

  • 01Your contact information.
  • 02A description of the copyrighted work or IP being infringed.
  • 03A direct link or clear identification of the infringing content.
  • 04A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the info is accurate and you are authorized to act.
  • 05Your reasoning to the takedown.

We typically respond to takedown requests within 72 hours. Bad-faith or fraudulent requests may be reported.
